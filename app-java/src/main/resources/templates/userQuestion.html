Explore Datasets with Natural Language

What can I ask?

This app is connected to a range of sample databases which you can select from, as follows:

Amazon Neptune - IMDb Graph

Movie information as described in this schema . Try asking questions about movies, their genres, and the artists that worked on them - for example:

  • Who's Quentin Tarantino's favourite actor?
  • How many movies has Robin Williams starred in by genre?
  • アンジェリーナジョリーの最初の映画はいつか？

Note that (despite our efforts...) the model often tries to traverse movie-Artist relations the wrong way e.g. (Artist)-[:actor]->(movie): Which is a common cause of complex queries returning empty results. For example, try "What movies did Angelina Jolie and Jonny Lee Miller star in together?"

Amazon RDS - Pagila DVD Rentals

Pagila is a Postgres port of the Sakila sample database described here , which is designed to represent a DVD rental store. Try asking questions like:

  • Show me the name of the 5 customers who rented the most dvds
  • Find the 5 actors whose movies have been rented the most
  • Welche Filme wurden am meisten ausgeliehen?

Amazon Athena - TPC-H supply chain

TPC-H is a supply-chain oriented business decision benchmarking dataset as described here , describing a business' suppliers and part orders. You can ask questions like:

  • Which 10 customers from ASEAN placed the most orders?
  • show me the top 10 suppliers from Jordan with highest sales
  • montrez-moi le top 5 des produits les plus achetés
